# Privacy Policy — Optiflow

**Effective date:** 2026-04-22

---

## Data we collect

This app does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.
We do not require account registration or login of any kind.

## Location

If you enable the GPS option for solar panel location, your device coordinates are used
**only** to request a solar irradiance forecast from a third-party weather service.
The coordinates are transmitted solely for that forecast request and are
**not** stored by us, logged, or used for any other purpose.

You can also enter your location manually — in that case GPS is never accessed.

## Electricity prices

The app fetches publicly available electricity price data from PSE (Polskie Sieci
Elektroenergetyczne) and ENTSO-E. No personal information is transmitted in these requests.

## Analytics and tracking

The app contains **no** analytics SDKs, crash-reporting libraries, advertising networks,
or any other form of tracking. We do not know who uses the app or how.

## Children

The app does not target children and does not collect data from any users regardless of age.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever add features that change how data is handled, this page will be updated and
the effective date above will change.
